---
title: "Placing your first bet"
---

SportToken offers odds in American style. Meaning each market will have the odds as +x and -x. +150 and -150 for example. -150 means that if you place 150 dollars you will win a 100 dollars and will be returned 250 dollars. +150 means that if you place 100 dollars you will win 150 dollars and will be returned 250 dollars.
