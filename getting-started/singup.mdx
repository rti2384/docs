## For non crypto users

We reccomend using the email option for signup. Simple select singup and enter your email. Insert the code that is sent to your email into the prompt and your all set\!

## For crypto users

You can connect your own self custodial crypto wallet to the site. Funds must be in USDC on the polygon network in order to actually place money on the site.
