We ensure that any bet placed by the Liquidity Pool is a Kelly Criterion Optimal. See [Kelly Criterion](https://www.investopedia.com/terms/k/kellycriterion.asp). This ensures that in the law of large numbers the Liquidity Pool will grow. Any bet a user takes charges a 0.3 system fee and a dynamic market fee. Both of which go towards the liquidity pool.
